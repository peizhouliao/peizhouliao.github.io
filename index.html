Welcome to my website! I received my PhD in Biostatistics from Emory University. I have been enjoying pushing forward the boundary of data science through the use of both statistics and machine learning techniques.

My research interests:
(1) Machine Learning;
(2) Data Mining;
(3) Recommender Systems.
